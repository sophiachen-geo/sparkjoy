How to buy

  1. Open an item → tap Email me. Include your name, the item, your preferred pickup day/time, and whether you'd want to ship instead.
  2. I'll reply within 24 hours to confirm the item is still available and agree on logistics.
  3. To hold the item: e-transfer 50% once I've confirmed. Use the item name in the e-transfer note.
  4. At pickup (or before shipping): e-transfer the remaining 50%, or pay the full amount via the Pay with PayPal button on the item — both work.

Payment recipients: Interac e-transfer to sophiazilisha@hotmail.com · PayPal to thegoldenratio.geo@icloud.com.

Pickup spots: any STM metro or REM station on the island of Montreal, or the SANAAQ or BANQ (Grande Bibliothèque) libraries.

Shipping: possible for some items — please email me first so we can discuss logistics and cost.

I'll be travelling within North America and Europe over the next few months — handoffs in those cities are sometimes possible. Just ask.

Contact
